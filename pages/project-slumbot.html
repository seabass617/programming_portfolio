Slumbot

Recycled Car Parts, Epoxy, MIG Welding

This project was driven by my passion for cars, recycled materials, and a curiosity for the connection between shapes. I sourced the scrap metal from a local junkyard and learned how to MIG weld to put this together. The piece has a few main components, the headlamp bulb eye, the wheel hub ears, crankshaft spine, and oil lines; the rest of the pieces were just parts that I had found interesting.

The most challenging and rewarding part of this project was figuring out how to place the intermediary pieces connecting the main components. The process of visualizing, and working the metal to help tell a believable story was incredibly rewarding.

Dream Multiverse Vacation

Technology: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Javscript, PostgresSQL, Pundit

Dream Multiverse Vacation is an Airbnb clone where you can buy and sell vacation getaways to different dimensions from the Rick and Morty Universe!

This was our first project for the Le Wagon coding bootcamp where we focused on learning the basics of creating a web marketplace and working on a team. I worked designing and implementing the front end. We wanted to make the experience as "Rick and Morty" as possible, so we included a lot space, future, and sci-fi themes, as well as little touches like a dimension icon for avatar profile pages and even allowing our guest to rent a "Morty" if they desired companionship on their vacation.

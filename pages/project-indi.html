Indigenius

Technology: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Javascript, PostgresSQL, PG_Search, Cloudinary

As the world becomes more globalized, there has been a rise in conscious consumers who want authentic goods and experiences. People don't want the "tourist trap" experience when they travel anymore. Nor do they want to buy a product that isn't actually supporting the culture it represents. Indigenius is a proposed solution to this problem. It works by connecting authentic artisans to ethical consumers around the globe in a quick, easy, and reliable manner.

There are two users for the website, the artisan, who can post products or experiences for sale, and the consumer, who is looking to purchase these products and experiences. An artisan has a custom profile where they can choose to post a product or an experience for sale. Details for the post can then be added, such as quanitiy, description, photos, dates, and location. A buyer can easily search through products or experience, select the quantity or duration (for workshops) to add to their cart, review their cart, and checkout seamlessly.

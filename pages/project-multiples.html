Armadillidiidae Hirudinea

Cast Cement

Armadillidiidae Hirudinea (pillbug leech) was a project focused on the idea of multiples. I was inspired by the Ohms from Hayao Miyazaki's film "Naussica of the Valley of the Wind." I loved seeing how there was so much emotion held within a creature that was essentially a slug with a bunch of eyes. I wanted to work on this idea of containing emotion within an insect type creature, something typically viewed by humans as not having feelings. Another aspect I wanted to play with was the idea of an insect colony and the different versions of what a community could look like. Which is why I decided to organize as if they were out feeding on the surrounding trees and brining nutrients back to their hive.

I originally sculpted the creature out of an oil based modeling clay, then created a mold out of silicon that would be supported by a plaster mother mold. I created four molds total and was able to quickly make 50 bugs.

Hi, my name is Sebastian. I'm a programmer living in Boston and I love creative coding, art, and science.

I’m a recent graduate of the Le Wagon coding bootcamp with a background in physics, studio art, and project management. I’m curious about programming as a form of creative expression and how it can be used to create surreal interactive experiences.

Before the bootcamp, I worked as a project engineer for a general contractor in Seattle and was an apprentice glassblower. In college, I programmed arduino controllers which helped model the oceanic flow of Europa, reconstructed and animated cane toad bones in Maya, and made dystopian robot heads out of recycled car parts. In my free time, I like to rock climb and rollerblade.

If you want to learn more about me, please take a look at my projects and cv.

I’m also currently on the job hunt looking for front end and creative coding opportunities!

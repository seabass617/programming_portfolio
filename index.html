Hi, my name is Sebastian. I'm a developer living in Boston and I love art, science, and creative coding.

about

I’m a recent graduate of the Le Wagon coding bootcamp with a background in project management, studio art, and physics. I’m curious about the relationship between digital experiences and the natural world and how these two can influence, interact, and inspire each other through space, time, and the senses.

Before the bootcamp, I worked as a project engineer for a general contractor in Seattle and was an apprentice glassblower. In college, I programmed arduino controllers which helped model the oceanic flow of Europa, reconstructed and animated cane toad bones in Maya, and made a dystopian robot head out of recycled car parts. In my free time, I like to rock climb, cook, and play guitar.

If you want to learn more about me, please take a look at my projects and cv.

I’m also currently on the job hunt looking for front end and creative coding opportunities!

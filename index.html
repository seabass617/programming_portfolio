Hi, my name is Sebastian. I'm a developer living in Boston and I love art, science, design, and programming.

about

I’m a recent graduate of the Le Wagon coding bootcamp with a background in project management, studio art, and physics. I’m curious about the relationship between man-made experiences and the natural world. I like to ask questions about how these two can influence, interact, and inspire each other through space, time, and the senses.

learn more

Before the bootcamp, I worked as a project engineer for a general contractor in Seattle and was an apprentice glassblower. In college, I programmed an arduino controller which helped model the oceanic flow of Europa, animated cane toad bones in Maya, and made a dystopian robot head out of recycled car parts. In my free time, I like to rock climb and play guitar.

If you want to learn more about me, please take a look at my projects and cv.
